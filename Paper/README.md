## Main result

In a matched Llama-3.1-8B/70B comparison, static animal-number output geometry
becomes less predictive at 70B while a natural assistant state gains causal
control earlier in relative depth. A separate Qwen study shows that pooling
variable-width decimal strings with per-token normalization can reverse the
sign of the measured association.

## Scope

The experiments characterize a frozen prompting channel. They do not establish
the mechanism of training-time trait transfer, a universal law across model
families, or a unique circuit. The supplement records the exact prompts,
estimands, controls, protocol amendments, and secondary outcomes behind those
boundaries.
