## Plain-English admin answer

- **AAAI does not require a professor endorsement.** You need a complete
  OpenReview author profile and every final coauthor listed by the deadline.
- **arXiv is separate and optional.** A first-time arXiv submission, or a first
  submission to a new category, can require endorsement. Start a draft arXiv
  submission to learn whether your account needs it. An institutional email may
  help; otherwise ask an active arXiv author in the field, such as a professor.
- **Do not send the same paper to AAAI and EACL/ARR at the same time.** The EACL
  2027 ARR deadline is August 3, 2026, so it is a fallback venue, not a parallel
  duplicate submission.
